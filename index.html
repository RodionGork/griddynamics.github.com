About Us


Grid Dynamics Cloud Practice formed in 2008. At that time we were the first company to run a public scalability benchmark of EC2 on 512 nodes. Since then, we have helped industry leaders, companies such as Cisco, eBay, Macy’s, Microsoft, and PayPal with their core cloud programs. Our achievements include:

  • Building a management system to operate 15,000 cloud nodes spread across 6 data centers;
  • Porting a boxed software application to SaaS capable of running on 10,000 cores;
  • Running 400 VM instances on OpenStack;
  • Implementing JClouds support for OpenStack.

Our Cloud Services Division uses expertise we gained from large-scale cloud implementations to provide a full range of services in cloud enablement of applications, platforms and internal processes.

Services


  • Applications and platforms

    — Transforming existing applications from boxed software to elastic, scalable, multitenant SaaS solutions;
    — Adjusting applications to leverage benefits of cloud computing;
    — Designing and implementing next-generation platforms for PaaS providers.

  • Private IaaS

    — Building private and hybrid clouds for enterprises;
    — One of top OpenStack contributors.

  • Internal Processes

    — Turnkey cloud solutions to streamline development process;
    — Integrating cloud environments into Quality Assurance;
    — CI solutions using public and private clouds.
